The Electronic voice phenomena (EVP) are recordings of voice or voice-like sounds that are not audible to the human ear. Shining a light on the unknown, possible paranormal claims to do with parapsychology. The art of recording EVPs, or electronic voice phenomena, is one of the most widely accepted methods of collecting evidence. The theory is that spirits do not have enough energy to create sounds audible to the human ear but can leave impressions on the tape. The EVP recordings within this webpage are recordings of investigators asking questions to said celebrities who have passed away and the responses that they get are either obvious or the soundwaves are up to the users interpretation. Some believe they are intentional hoaxes or definitive proof of life after death.
